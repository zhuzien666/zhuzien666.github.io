Research Interests

    I am mainly interested in Condensed Matter Physics Theory, Computation and Simulation.
    In specific, I'm focusing on:
  • non-equilibrium growth process and physical properties of low-dimensional systems and quantum materials, etc.
    • Other topics that I am interested in (but not in research currently)
  • The formation, stability and evolution of singularities in compressible fluids.
  • Long time solution of (compressible) fluids, e.g., small data long time wellposedness, stability of certain equilibria.
